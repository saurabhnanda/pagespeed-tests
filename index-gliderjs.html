    Are you ready to leave behind beaches and nightlife to explore a side of Goa not many know and talk of?

    A side of Goa rich with culture, heritage, and biodiversity, delicious local food, vibrant backwaters, and unexplored islands awaits! Soul Travelling specializes in offbeat tourism. We curate immersive trails  with unique experience points in them.

    It's time to Discover Goa's Best Kept Secrets with Soul Travelling!

    Pick Your Concept

    Walking Tours / Heritage Walks

    Walking tours provide a deeper understanding of the local culture, history, and lifestyle.  Goa is popular as a coastal paradise for its beaches, but in a broader perspective it’s a treasure trove of historical epics and heritage monuments.  From Panjim to Margao explore Heritage homes with Portuguese style architecture, old mansions and churches. Our walking tours in Goa are led by local Goans for an authentic and sterling experience.

    Village Excursions

    Tranquility is the essence of rural tourism that one is sure to find in village visits. Exploring rural India is bliss. It’s an altered experience from urban life.  We always insist on the fact that there is no better way to understand a place, its people and the local culture than getting to know it from the locals themselves. Goan villages channel abundance of serenity. Dive into the rural part of Goa and discover a whole new world. 

    Culinary Experiences and Food Trails

    Goa’s cuisine is a blend of taste and tradition. What’s notable about the traditional Goan food is its remarkable fusion of Portuguese, Konkani and other regional flavours.  In the hunt for the most famous food in Goa many miss out on the wide variety of Goan dishes and settle for ‘fish curry and rice’. We bet that’s not enough exploration. The best way to actually understand the Goan culinary scene would be to indulge in hands-on cooking demos or joining in food trails. 

    Nature and Wildlife

    The state of Goa is blessed with abundance of natural resources and anchors a rich biodiversity, proffering a mesmerizing world of wildlife to explore.  With numerous wildlife sanctuaries, mangroves, forests the captivating western ghats proves to be a myriad of flora and fauna.  Exploring wildlife in Gao can be an exciting adventure for nature and wildlife enthusiasts. In fact it’s a heaven for bird watching. The boat ride, sunrise views, sunset points, ferry rides, and natural pools are fun surprises while nature tours.

    North Goa Trails

    While beaches in Goa are alluring, there is so much more to explore. North Goa offers abundant offbeat experiences that go beyond beaches.  The charm of North Goa doesn’t really end at its nightlife and parties, In fact it lies past it. There are many more activities to do in North Goa that are not beach hopping. Exploring the island of Divar, enjoying nature trail at Chorao, walking in the vibrant lanes of Fontainhas or diving into the history and exploring heritage of Old Goa are some of the not to miss extraordinary experiences.  These are only to name a few, one can always get more adventurous with experiences like mud bathing, treasure hunts or a tavern trail.

    South Goa Trails

    South Goa experiences, waterfalls, nature trails, nature and wildlife, spice plantations, rock carvings, traditional bakeries, caves, places to visit in a day.  A quaint side of Goa awaits, away from the crowd with a blend of natural beauty and cultural richness. South Goa is an epitome of serenity and plethora of surprises.  Be it uniqueness of age old traditional bakeries or stunning rock carvings and caves, South Goa is sure to appease the wanderer in you. It’s no less than a paradise for travellers when it comes to offbeat activities - waterfall hopping, spice plantation visit, village excursions South Goa has it all.

    The Soul Travelling Family

    Varun Hegde

    • Varun Hegde
    • Co-Founder

    Its the man himself, the first Soul Traveller. Varun tells us about what inspired him to start Soul Travelling and the road ahead. Can't believe it's been a year already. I still remember that day on 12th November. The first ever Margao City Walk. I'd put in a lot of effort for curating it, but went easy on the promotions. Only 3 people turned up, but it was a lot of fun showing them around Margao. In the second walk, we focused more on promotions - and we had 26 people! This time we had to learn - about managing crowd. It's been a delightful journey since then. Born and brought up in Goa, it has a special place in my heart. I did my engineering, and then worked for 4 long years in the IT industry. My last job was in Frankfurt. I had an amazing time travelling the lengths and breaths of Europe, and realized that travel was a part of life in the European culture. People there dont just travel for sightseeing. Exploring new cultures, traditions, cuisines, meeting new people are some of the reasons that drive European travellers. That is where the idea of Soul Travelling was born. In Goa, you have a very interesting Baga-Calangute model. You are bombarded with questions the moment you say you're from Goa - the stereotypes exist. So the time is ripe to break the stereotypes and provide an alternate model for Tourism - and that is what Soul Travelling intends to do - Change the way people perceive travel in Goa and rest of India.
